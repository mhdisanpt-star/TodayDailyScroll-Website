Product

Core modules for daily shorts, weekly compilations, and long-form workflows.

Human review queue, quality gates, and regeneration when an output needs revision.

Publishing

YouTube and TikTok upload with verification, retry logic, and attempt history.

Operations

Pipeline status, dataset expansion progress, queues, and health alerts.

Analytics

Channel metrics, monetization progress, and post-publish performance data.

Benchmark

Compare editorial structure against reference videos. Knowledge requires operator approval.

Learning

Separate systems for human feedback, production metrics, and benchmark patterns.

Workflow

From planning to a published asset, with review at each stage.

  1. Plan — Select topics and apply editorial patterns.
  2. Produce — Generate scripts, hooks, and renders through the pipeline.
  3. Review — Approve or send back for revision.
  4. Publish — Upload to connected platforms with verification.
  5. Track — Monitor performance and channel health.
